Cat Species

The Felidae or cat Family is currently classed as having 41 species. The family includes 40 wild cat species, and also our beloved domestic pets.In 2003, the International Commission on Zoological Nomenclature ruled that the domestic cat is a distinct species, namely Felis catus.

Cat Nature

Cats are often considered aloof or unaffectionate, but that is a misunderstanding of their nature. They are solitary hunters but are nevertheless social animals.Their behavior is a mix of natural instincts, learned behaviors, and individual personality traits.
